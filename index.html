Our Story

Founded in 2025, Mark's Boba Bonanza was born from a passion for authentic Taiwanese milk tea. Our founder, Mark, brought her family's secret recipes from Taipei to share with the world.

We source only the finest tea leaves and ingredients, ensuring every cup delivers the perfect balance of flavor, aroma, and texture that milk tea lovers crave.

Mark's Boba Bonanza, we believe in the art of tea-making and the joy it brings to people's lives. Every sip is crafted with care and tradition.
